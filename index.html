Welcome to Caliphate Capital Group.

Caliphate Capital Group is a Limited Liability Company registered in Bahrain with the financial and technical backing of some offshore companies and investors. We have behind us years of experience in investment activities. We share the Government vision to develop Bahrain with a view to opening up the region to global business and empowering the people.


Caliphate Capital Group Limited has grown to become a leader in investments, loans and public works contracting, together with real estate development. Over the years the Company has acquired broad expertise in its fields of work. Its qualified staff and up-to-date techniques, supported by extensive experience allow the Company to deliver works of high quality and enable the client to obtain high value for their money.

Caliphate Capital Group Limited was founded with talented members of the finance profession in order to operate in the fields of public works, financial services and real estate development, and was structured to be able to operate with partners possessing complementary skills. This pragmatic approach has enabled the Company to develop into a most dynamic investment firms.
