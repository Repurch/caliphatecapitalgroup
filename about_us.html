About

Caliphate Capital Group is a Limited Liability Company registered in Bahrain with the financial and technical backing of some offshore companies and investors. We have behind us years of experience in investment activities. We share the Government vision to develop Bahrain with a view to opening up the region to global business and empowering the people.
Caliphate Capital Group Limited has grown to become a leader in investments, loans and public works contracting, together with real estate development. Over the years the Company has acquired broad expertise in its fields of work. Its qualified staff and up-to-date techniques, supported by extensive experience allow the Company to deliver works of high quality and enable the client to obtain high value for their money.
Caliphate Capital Group Limited was founded with talented members of the finance profession in order to operate in the fields of public works, financial services and real estate development, and was structured to be able to operate with partners possessing complementary skills. This pragmatic approach has enabled the Company to develop into a most dynamic investment firms.
In real estate, the Company has grown into one of the leading contractor for turnkey projects. The Company can undertake projects from conception to completion, delivering them to their clients completed and in running condition. The Company management goals are forthright: on-time delivery, state- of- the –art material and workmanship, compliance with international regulations and specifications, and for doing so the Company is constantly increasing its technical capabilities, its cost effectiveness and its staff selected with great care.
To guarantee customer satisfaction, the Company has assembled an outstanding collection of specialized offline and web-based machinery as well as more versatile equipment. This flexibility allows the Company to always win the trust of its clients.
Finally the Company’s financial credentials have always been entirely satisfactory. With the seriousness, competence and high moral standards of its Partners and Directors, Caliphate Capital Group has always fulfilled its business commitments.

GROUP MANAGEMENT BOARD.


Caliphate Capital Group works in conjunction with Various international banks, this organization is created to serve the needs of those investors or borrowers who are seeking for long-term capital growth and high levels of asset protection from downward global market movements, Caliphate Capital Group is a business finance outlet and a third party funds clearing house and intermediary that facilitates private business transactions and provides consulting services to businesses and individual private matters.
Caliphate Capital Group has been in business and serving clients globally through its transit offices in Asia and Europe, in addition to other countries way back, we offer a wide array of financial services on our investors variety of funds, including Invoice Factoring; we can assist on offshore account opening, equipment leasing, finance, across border finance, business appraisal and management.
Our management of financial assets embodies proven stock selection skills and intricate research processes to generate superior returns at lower than market risk through the consistent application of our low volatility fund management philosophy, we believe that the rigorous application of this philosophy provides unrivaled products and services that completely satisfy the investment needs of our clients, this has been proven through the consistency of our excellent performance and the many accolades we have received over the years.
Our Group Management Board meets frequently and operates as a general management committee under the direct authority of the Board. The objective of the Group Management Board is to maintain a reporting and control structure whereby all of the line operations of Caliphate Capital Group are accountable to individual members of the Group Management Board, who reports to the Group Chief Executive who also in turn reports to the Group Chairman. The Board has set objectives and measures for the Group Management Board. These align senior executives' objectives and measures with the strategy and operating plans throughout Caliphate Capital Group.
Our Funding Group does not only provide access to finances, deal structuring and sector technical expertise, but also advisory services, project development capacity and funding, to bridge the infrastructure investments and access deficits in the core infrastructure sectors of power, natural resources, heavy industry, transport and telecommunications, all critical pillars for economic growth across the globe which does not lead to deterioration in asset qualities.
Caliphate Capital Group is again, the preferred provider of targeted financial services globally based on good and reliable strong customer relationships and our financial strong base, we will strengthen these relationships by providing the right solutions that combine our technology, expertise and financial strength, our goal is to create customer loyalty in terms of strong funding of our network clients, shareholder value and employee satisfaction, we will be the best run, Customer-Focused, Integrated Financial provider, with a Unique and Inclusive Customer Culture.
